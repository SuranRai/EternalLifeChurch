Nestled in the heart of Fort Worth, Texas, Eternal Life Church is the Christ-Centered Nepali community church, it stands as a beacon of faith and cultural richness. Founded with the mission to create a home away from home for Nepali Christians, this vibrant church has become a spiritual haven for the Nepali community in the greater Fort Worth area.

ABOUT US

Established on February, 9, 2014, by a dedicated individuals Pastor Raj Tamang and his family from their appartment. Eternal Life Church was born out of a desire to provide a place where Nepali Christians could worship, connect, and grow in their faith while maintaining the cultural ties that bind them together. Beyond religious gatherings, Eternal Life Church is actively involved in community outreach. The church members engage in various charitable activities, partnering with local organizations to address the needs of the broader Fort Worth community. This commitment to service not only reflects the teachings of Christ but also embodies the Nepali spirit of compassion and community. Worship services at the church are an eclectic mix of Nepali and English, fostering a sense of unity among the diverse congregation. The vibrant music, infused with traditional Nepali tunes, creates an atmosphere of joy and praise. The sermons, delivered by the dedicated pastors, intertwine biblical teachings with the unique cultural perspectives of the Nepali community, making the messages relatable and impactful.

OUR MISSION & VISION

At the core of the Eternal Life Church's vision lies the unwavering commitment to "reach, teach, equip, and send" in accordance with the profound guidance found in John 10:28.
Reaching: The church seeks to extend the transformative love of Christ to individuals within and beyond the Nepali community. Inspired by John 10:28, it believes that every reached soul is securely held by the Good Shepherd's eternal embrace.
Teaching: Rooted in biblical principles, the church aims to deepen members' understanding of God's Word. Emphasizing Christ's teachings, it empowers individuals to live purposefully, guided by eternal truths found in the scriptures.
Equipping: Recognizing the importance of spiritual growth, the church dedicates itself to equipping members with tools for a resilient faith. Through discipleship programs and mentorship, it prepares individuals to navigate life's challenges while staying grounded in their Christian identity.
Sending: Embracing a mission-oriented spirit, the church envisions sending members as ambassadors of Christ. Whether locally or globally, it believes in the transformative power of individuals living out their faith, spreading the message of love and salvation.
