MISSION & VISION

In the heart of a vibrant community, stands the Eternal Life Church, a beacon of light guided by the profound words of John 10:28 - "I give them eternal life, and they shall never perish; no one will snatch them out of my hand." Embracing this promise, the church has dedicated itself to a mission and vision that revolve around reaching, teaching, equipping, and sending. Inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ, the church is committed to reaching out to all, irrespective of background or circumstance. The congregation strives to embody Christ's love by fostering a welcoming environment that embraces diversity and encourages spiritual growth.

The church actively extends outreach through evangelism, sharing Christ's transformative message and offering. It prioritizes teaching the Gospel truth through sermons, study groups, and education, deepening members' understanding and fostering spiritual maturity.

Committed to the Great Commission, the church envisions sending equipped disciples as ambassadors of God's love, extending the reach of God's kingdom in local ministries or global missions and embodying the transformative power of eternal life.

OUR BRANCHES

ELC is dedicated to spreading love, compassion, and spiritual guidance to communities around the globe. Our mission involves establishing branches in Columbus, Ohio; Shippensburg, Pennsylvania; and Rupa, India, with a vision to create inclusive spaces for people to connect, grow spiritually, and contribute positively to society.
